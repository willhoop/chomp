## Key takeaways

- **In rain, your swimmers outrun everything** — Basculegion 260 and Swampert 244 clear even Mega Aero (222) and Scarf Bascu (214). Rain up = you move first.
- **Rain mirrors:** weather is field-wide — opposing Swift Swim Basculegion doubles under your rain too. Their Scarf version (390) beats yours (260); Mystic Water/Life Orb mirrors are a speed tie. Counterplay: Fake Out, Aqua Jet, or stack Tailwind (520).
- **Raichu at 178** outspeeds Jolly Garchomp and Charizard but ties Mega Metagross (speed roll) and loses to Sneasler and opposing Whimsicott. Fake Out covers Sneasler leads.
- **No rain = you're slow.** Basculegion drops to 130, under Garchomp/Zard. Preserve Pelipper and Whimsicott's Rain Dance vs sun — your speed profile lives on weather.
- **Trick Room flips you hard:** four of six are fast. If TR goes up, Incineroar (80) and Pelipper (97) are your best pieces. Vs non-Farigiraf setters (Sinistcha etc.), Encore stops it; vs Farigiraf, see the game plan below.

## Farigiraf TR game plan

**You cannot stop the setup** — Armor Tail blanks Fake Out, Taunt, Encore, and all Prankster moves while Farigiraf (or its ally) is on the field. No Encore on Farigiraf, period. But **Trick Room is -7 priority: Farigiraf always moves dead last on the turn it sets.** Kill it before TR resolves.

1. **Lead Basculegion + Raichu, double-target the giraffe turn 1.** Farigiraf is 120 HP but ~70/70 defenses: Life Orb rain Wave Crash does ~85–100%, and mega Raichu's turn-1 terrain doubles Rising Voltage to finish. Don't bother with Fake Out — it's blocked for both their slots.
2. **If they Protect it**, hit the partner instead and repeat next turn. They can't Protect forever.
3. **If TR goes up anyway**, don't swing with fast mons — Swampert Protects key turns, Pelipper Wide Guards spread damage, Incineroar pivots. It's 5 turns and their abusers still eat rain Wave Crash.
4. **Once Farigiraf is gone**, Whimsicott's whole kit turns back on.

## Matchup corrections worth remembering

- **Kingambit is 96% Defiant** — never Intimidate-cycle or Parting Shot into it. Answers: Close Combat, High Horsepower, Encore-lock Sucker Punch, rain Wave Crash.
- **Farigiraf is Normal/Psychic — immune to Last Respects.** Break it with rain-boosted Wave Crash; Dark (Throat Chop tech) is its only 2x weakness. It blanks Fake Out, Aqua Jet, and all Prankster moves (Tailwind/Encore) for its whole side.
- **Sinistcha** — resists Water, immune to Fake Out/Close Combat. Hurricane and Last Respects are 2x.
