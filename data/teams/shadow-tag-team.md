# SHADOW TAG TEAM — Reg M-B

*Built 7/20/2026 from `reg-mb-threat-list.md` (141,499 battles), `mainline_megas.json`, `champions-status-rules.md`. Every mon below is confirmed present in Reg M-B — either on your own teams or in the logged threat list.*

**Identity:** trap both opponents, take away their choices, then break them. Fun comes from denial, not raw damage.

---

## The team

**Gengar-Mega** @ Gengarite — Shadow Tag — Timid — 4 HP / 252 SpA / 252 Spe
Shadow Ball / Sludge Bomb / Hypnosis / Protect
> 200 Spe after mega — outruns Sneasler (189), loses only to Mega Aerodactyl (222). Pre-mega it's Cursed Body; **Shadow Tag only comes online the turn you mega.** 170 SpA is the highest offensive stat in the mega list.

**Whimsicott** @ Focus Sash — Prankster — Timid — 252 HP / 4 SpA / 252 Spe
Encore / Taunt / Tailwind / Moonblast
> The lock half of the combo. Encore is +1 priority, so it lands before the target moves.

**Meowscarada** @ Life Orb — Protean — Jolly — 252 Atk / 4 SpD / 252 Spe
Fake Out / Flower Trick / Knock Off / Sucker Punch
> 192 Spe. Fake Out buys the mega turn. Flower Trick always crits — ignores Stamina/Cosmic Power boosts.

**Kingambit** @ Black Glasses — Defiant — Adamant — 252 HP / 252 Atk / 4 SpD
Kowtow Cleave / Sucker Punch / Iron Head / Protect
> **The Ghost answer, and the Sinistcha answer.** Kowtow Cleave is Dark, 2× on Sinistcha (28.1% usage, your worst matchup). Also immune to enemy Prankster status.

**Farigiraf** @ Assault Vest — Armor Tail — Relaxed — 252 HP / 4 Atk / 252 SpD
Psychic Noise / Hyper Voice / Helping Hand / Trick Room
> **Armor Tail blanks enemy priority for your whole side** — no Fake Out, no Sucker Punch, no Extreme Speed. That's what protects slow Gengar setup turns. Immune to Last Respects (Normal).

**Sinistcha** @ Leftovers — Heatproof — Calm — 252 HP / 4 SpA / 252 SpD
Rage Powder / Strength Sap / Matcha Gotcha / Trick Room
> Rage Powder redirects everything off Gengar while it sets up. Resists Ground — your Garchomp cushion (29.5% usage).

## Combo lines

**The lock (turn 1–2):**
1. Lead Gengar + Whimsicott. Mega on turn 1 → Shadow Tag active, neither opponent can switch.
2. Whimsicott Encore on whatever they clicked — Protect, Fake Out, a setup move, anything.
3. That mon is now trapped *and* locked into one move for 3 turns. Kill the other one at leisure.

**The Taunt variant:** vs Trick Room / Tailwind / screens leads, Taunt beats the setter to the punch and Shadow Tag means they can't pivot out of it.

**The slow turn:** Sinistcha Rage Powder + Gengar Hypnosis. Everything aims at Sinistcha, which just Strength Saps it back.

**Speed inversion:** you have two Trick Room setters (Farigiraf, Sinistcha). Against a faster team, flip it and Kingambit becomes the fastest thing on the field.

## Honest weaknesses — read before laddering

1. **Ghost-types ignore trapping entirely.** Sinistcha (28.1%), Basculegion (24.0%), Gholdengo (8.6%), Annihilape, Houndstone — all walk out of Shadow Tag freely. That's a big slice of the meta. Against Ghost leads, Shadow Tag is dead weight and you win on the Kingambit/Farigiraf half instead. **This is the team's real ceiling.**
2. **Mega Gengar is 60/80/95.** It dies to anything that connects. Fake Out into a Kingambit Sucker Punch ends it. Farigiraf is not optional insurance — it's the reason the mega survives.
3. **You cannot Intimidate.** Deliberate: Kingambit is 25.2% usage with 96% Defiant. No Intimidate on this team is a feature.
4. **Whimsicott's Prankster fails into Dark** — Kingambit, Grimmsnarl, Hydreigon, Incineroar, Sableye. Encore does nothing to any of them. Kingambit is your answer to your own dead button.
5. **One mega, item clause.** Gengarite is the whole mega slot — no Blaziken or Swampert backup.

## Bring-4 guide

| They show | Bring | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Ghost-heavy (Sinistcha / Basculegion / Gholdengo) | Kingambit, Farigiraf, Meowscarada, Sinistcha | Trapping is off. Play the Dark/priority-block game |
| Trick Room (Farigiraf / Torkoal / Mawile) | Whimsicott, Gengar, Meowscarada, Kingambit | Taunt the setter, trap it so it can't escape |
| Fast offense (Aerodactyl / Sneasler / Staraptor) | Farigiraf, Sinistcha, Kingambit, Gengar | Flip Trick Room, Armor Tail blanks their priority |
| Rain (Pelipper / Basculegion / Swampert) | Kingambit, Farigiraf, Whimsicott, Meowscarada | Basculegion escapes anyway; out-prioritize instead |
| Kingambit visible | Gengar, Whimsicott, Farigiraf, Sinistcha | Never Encore it, never Intimidate it, trap and burst it |
