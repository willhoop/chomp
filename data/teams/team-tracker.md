## Standing lessons (violate at your peril)

1. **Bring-4 by matchup, not autopilot.** Lure trio only when their Gambit is likely to come. Blaziken vs TTar/sand. **Ghold is MANDATORY vs Staraptor teams.**
2. **Never HJK into a probable Protect** (fresh switch-in, telegraphed nuke). Taunt first or target elsewhere.
3. **Mega Staraptor inverts stat drops** — Intimidate/Charm/CC drops all boost it post-mega. Don't feed it.
4. **Foul Play eats your big-Atk mons** — Grimmsnarl/Farigiraf Foul Play punish Tsareena (189 Atk) and hit Ghold 2x. Trade with Ghold/Whimsi into Foul Play users.
5. **Contact into Baneful Bunker = poison.** Check before Fake Out/Flare Blitz vs Pex.
6. **Sucker Punch discipline:** vs low-HP mons behind likely Protect, click Kowtow.
7. Their Gambit keeps staying home at preview vs this team — lure deterrence is real; plan for 4v5 on their side.
