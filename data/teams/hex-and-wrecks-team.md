## Why each piece
- **Mega Scovillain (Spicy Spray)** — verified: burns anything it damages. Flamethrower = damage + guaranteed burn; Burning Jealousy is spread AND burns anything that boosted that turn (Contrary/Bulk Up punish). 138/138 offenses after mega.
- **Chandelure (Flame Body)** — the Hex nuke: 130 BP STAB into anything statused, Life Orb on 145 SpA. Flame Body burns contact attackers as a bonus spreader. Mega goes to Scovillain, so Chandelure keeps its base ability.
- **Rotom-Wash** — the patch piece the engine demanded: Levitate (fixes the EQ AllySwitch nightmare), Hydro Pump (sun/birds), non-Prankster Will-O-Wisp that **works on Dark-types** — your Kingambit answer.
- **Sableye (Prankster)** — priority dual status + Taunt for TR denial. Mental Herb eats the first Taunt aimed at it.
- **Sinistcha (Hospitality)** — Matcha Gotcha (burn + drain), second Hex, Strength Sap (stall fuel off their Attack stat), Rage Powder to shield Annihilape's Bulk Up turns. Resists EQ.
- **Annihilape (Defiant)** — the boost-sweep wincon: Rage Fist grows off every burn-chip hit it takes, Bulk Up behind redirection, Drain Punch sustains. Intimidate feeds it.

## Status-immunity coverage grid
Fire-types (no burn) → Toxic-chip? No Toxic here — para (TWave) + Hydro Pump. Electric-types (no para) → burn. Poison/Steel (no toxic) → burn/para. Ground (TWave-immune) → burn. Dark (Prankster-immune) → **Rotom/Chandelure Will-O-Wisp or Scovillain contact**, never Sableye.

## Traps to respect (from your own banned list)
- Sableye's ENTIRE kit fails on Dark foes (Prankster) — vs Kingambit/Malamar teams, Rotom is the status lead.
- Spirit-Break-style stat drops are gone from this team on purpose — nothing feeds Contrary Staraptor except Burning Jealousy damage (safe).
- Misty Terrain (Mega Audino) and Safeguard shut the whole plan off — Taunt the setter or kill it first.
- Worst matchup: Dedicated TR (7.75) — Taunt Sableye must survive turn 1; lead Sableye+Sinistcha there.
