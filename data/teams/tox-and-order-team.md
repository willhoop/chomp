You were right: 4 of 6 were Ghost-typed and Hex is blanked by Normal-types. v2 keeps the status engine but moves the damage to **poison abuse** — only one half-Ghost remains. Ghost/Dark spam now gets bodied instead of bodying you: Overqwil resists both, Annihilape's Fighting STAB deletes Dark AND Normal.

## The status engine
- **Burn**: every Mega Scovillain hit (Spicy Spray, verified) + Burning Jealousy spread that also punishes Bulk Up/Contrary boosters.
- **Toxic-poison**: Toxapex Toxic never misses (Poison-type rule), Barb Barrage adds 50% poison per click, Poison Point punishes contact on two mons (Qwil, Dragalge-style chip via Rocky Helmet Chomp).
- **Paralysis**: Rotom-Wash Thunder Wave — deliberately NOT on a Prankster so it lands on Kingambit and every other Dark.

## The abusers
- **Venoshock** doubles to 130 on poisoned targets; **Merciless** makes every Toxapex hit a crit on them.
- **Barb Barrage** doubles to 120 physical on poisoned targets — Overqwil both spreads and cashes in.
- **Rage Fist** grows off all the chip your statuses force through; Bulk Up + Drain Punch behind Baneful Bunker/para support is the sweep-or-stall wincon.

## Anti-meta answers baked in
Ghost spam (Bascu/Ghold/Ceruledge): Overqwil resists + Crunch. Dark spam (Kingambit): Ape Drain Punch, Rotom TWave/burn... and nothing on this team is Prankster-dependent. Normal bulk (Farigiraf/Kanga/Maushold): Ape Fighting STAB, no Hex reliance. TR: Overqwil Taunt (fast, and Dark — their Prankster can't Taunt HIM first). EQ teams: Rotom Levitate + your own Chomp outclicking them. Sun: Rotom Hydro + Chomp Rock Slide.

## Leads (default)
- Standard: **Rotom + Scovillain** (TWave/burn turn 1, Protect the right side), Ape + Pex in back.
- Vs TR: **Overqwil + Rotom** (Taunt setter, TWave abusers).
- Vs Zard/sun: **Rotom + Chomp**.
